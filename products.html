Where It Fits
應用位置

Valve placement across the cooling loop

閥件於冷卻迴路中的配置

  • Electric control valve — large-flow isolation, zone shutoff, fail-close/fail-open, total flow regulation.
  • 電動控制閥 — 大流量隔離、分區關斷、故障關閉／開啟、總流量調節。
  • Motor valve — closed-loop feedback, high-precision flow modulation, <2s full stroke.
  • 電動閥 — 閉迴路回授控制、高精度流量調節,全行程 <2 秒。
  • Solenoid valve — branch fast open/close, fault isolation, <30ms actuation.
  • 電磁閥 — 分支快速開關、故障隔離,動作時間 <30 毫秒。
  • Needle valve — venting, sampling, calibration, fine tuning.
  • 針閥 — 排氣、取樣、校正、微調。

Pump

泵浦

Canned Motor Pump (CMP)

罐式馬達泵浦(CMP)

Seal-less design isolates the motor from the fluid path, removing the dynamic shaft seal that limits traditional pump life.

無軸封設計將馬達與流體路徑隔離,移除限制傳統泵浦壽命的動態軸封。
